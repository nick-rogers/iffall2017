2017

Lynn Hill's career has been a resounding success. She won over 15 major competitions and dozens of first ascents. In recent years she has continued to participate in her passion and has served as a sponsor for Patagonia clothing line.

1994

Lynn free-climbed "The Nose," of the El Capitan mountain in Yosemite Valley. She was the first to complete this climb in under 24 hours. The face of the mountain is over 2,900 ft high.

1986

Lynn's acheivements drew the attention of the French Alpine club, who invited her to climb in France. These were her first experiences with competition climbing and she excelled in the new environment.

1975

Lynn first experienced mountain climbing at the age of 16. She went on trips to Yosemite National Park, where she climbed with the Camp 4 group. While there, Lynn joined the park's search and rescue team.
